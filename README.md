Desmos Bossfight:
A fight between an entity and a player with a weapon. While the player can only launch bullets that do 10 damage, the entity has various attacks all based off of math. The boss will have two phases, at the first phase, he will have 5000 hp and will have 3 easy attacks that will repeat until the player gets to phase two, the player will need to shoot the entity until he reaches 0 hp, which will unlock the next phase (the entity will miraculously be at 1hp). In the second phase, the entity will have not only 3 harder attacks that repeat but also a shield around him with 100 hp, to break it, the player will need to answer the math question on it to be able to damage it, and then shoot it 10 times. The entity will summon 10 shields over phase 2. After all 10 shields are destroyed, the player will finally be able to shoot the entity and win. However, if the player’s hp reaches 0 at any time within the fight, he will lose. Also, the player has a dash ability that can be activated with q; all damage will be negated during the dash

What tech will you use?
I will use pygame to draw the entity, player, and attacks. I will use .random to surprise the player and make them react. I will use classes to allow for things like bullets to be replicated easier. Lastly, I will use math for functions like atan2, which will let the player face at the mouse, sin and cos, which will allow for rotated motion, and vectors, which allow for offsets
